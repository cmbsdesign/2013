In October of 2011, Threadless held a compeition to design a shirt for certain colleges. I was excited that UCLA was a part of the select group to participate in the compeition and I decided to enter. These were my two submissions. The first design is called Paint It Gold and the inspiration behind it is UCLA's Blue and Gold week which is the week before the first football game against cross-town rival USC. The second design is called Teddy Bruins with Josie and Joe Bruin as Teddy Bears.
