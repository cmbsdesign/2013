This is the logo that I designed for D23's celebration of EPCOT's 30th anniversary. I knew from the beginning that I wanted to incorporate Spaceship Earth in the logo because that's the centerpiece of EPCOT. The monorail came in after I thinking about what other elements of "the future" can be put into it. I also helped design some merchandise for the event commemorating the attractions Horizons and Kitchen Kabaret. To say the least, it was great to work on something that celebrated Walt Disney's vision of the future.
