AT D23 EXPO 2013

This is a project that I helped design for a once-in-a-lifetime concert put on by Disney Legends Richard M. Sherman and Alan Menken at the D23 Expo 2013. My job was to develop a look and feel for the concert and lay out images that show up on screen while the performers sang their beloved songs from Disney animated and live action films, television shows, Broadway plays, and theme park attractions. The inspiration of the look and feel for the concert came from early Disney animated films like Make Mine Music and Melody Time.
