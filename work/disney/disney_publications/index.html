These are examples of some of the issues from the various publications that D23: The Official Disney Fan Club has worked on between 2012-2013. Disney twenty-three is a quarterly magazine issued to gold level members of the fan club. Most of the time I assist with image clipping or cleaning. For the Spring 2013 I had the chance to work on the cover by designing the twenty-three masthead to look like the logo for the film Oz The Great and Powerful. And for the Summer 2013 issue, I helped design a special cover that commemorates the 60th anniversary of Walt Disney Imagineering in the style of Disney artists Kevin Kidney and Jody Daily.
